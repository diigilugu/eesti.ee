    • 2022-02-11 16:56

      Keep in mind! A European Union COVID recovery certificate cannot be created based on a rapid antigen test. A recovery certificate can only be created with a confirmed PCR test and it can be done starting from the 11th day after receiving the result. The time the test was done is also important – exactly 11 days must have passed (11x24h).

    • 2021-11-02 10:03

      At the national eBooking system, you can book, change and cancel if necessary, the appointment time for the first dose of COVID-19 vaccination, the second dose and also the booster dose according to the time offers of the medical institutions.
      The timing of vaccination with the booster dose for people with immunodeficiency will be agreed with your doctor.
      You can book an appointment for the COVID-19 vaccination by calling 1247 or by contacting the reception of another nearby healthcare provider.
      You can find the nearest vaccination place on the vaktsineeri.ee website Getting a vaccination.

    Additional information of cookies

    We place so-called cookies in the browser(s) of your device (computer, smart device, etc.) in order for the patient portal to work properly, analyze its use and provide the best user experience.

    The cookie itself is a small text file that contains information that is used to identify the user and improve the user experience of the website.

    Cookies make it possible to ensure that the Patient Portal better meets your needs, for example, that it is better to find the necessary information on the website and that the use of the website is secure.

    Cookies do not read or transmit information from the user's device.


    Essential cookies

    These cookies allow you to navigate our website, do not collect information about you that could be used for marketingpurposes, and do not remember where you have been online. The website will not function properly without these cookies, so these cookies cannot be disabled.
